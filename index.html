My Sister’s Birthday

To my sweet sister, my lovely sister, Kore with the vibes, Queen Lateefah, I am so glad that you are my sister. The greatest gift our parents ever gave us was each other.

On this special day of yours, I wish you more of what you wish yourself. May all your heart’s desires come true. May your life be filled with love, health, and happiness.

May the day bring you good news. The oil of grace upon your life will never run dry. Just like the eagle, you will soar higher. Like the palm tree, you will flourish. Like gold, you will be valued. Like the sun, you shall shine bright.

Thank you for everything—your advice, encouragement, financial support, and motivation. I’m so grateful for everything, my lovely sister. I love you to the moon and back.
